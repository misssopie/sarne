VITRO

VITRO, Inc. is the data center subsidiary of PLDT Inc. that operates advanced data centers across the Philippines, including the first data center in Mindanao located in Davao City. During our visit, I observed that VITRO provides services such as internet access, data center interconnection, and disaster recovery seats, all supported by a highly secured and well-maintained facility. I learned that VITRO focuses on strong infrastructure and multi-layered data protection to ensure continuous operations and keep client data safe even during emergencies.

JAIROSOFT

911

The Davao City Central Communications and Emergency Response Center, also known as Central 911, provides primary emergency services such as medical assistance, search and rescue, fire auxiliary, and K-9 operations for the residents of Davao City. During our visit, I observed their advanced monitoring systems, including CCTV, eagle-eye surveillance, river and weather monitoring, and well-organized command centers that operate 24/7. I learned that by using both international and local monitoring systems, Central 911 is able to detect disasters early and reduce their impact through effective early warning, evacuation, and emergency response measures.
